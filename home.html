Overview

Amrapali Group of Institutes, Haldwani Admission 2023: Admission Process, Placement and Scholarship

Amrapali Group of Institutes is a private college famous for its undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Hotel Management, Pharmacy Applied Sciences and Management. The courses are offered in four different institutions -

  • Amrapali Institute of Hotel Management
  • Amrapali Institute of Pharmacy and Sciences
  • Amrapali Institute of Technology and Sciences
  • Amrapali Institute of Applied Sciences

Amrapali Group of Institutes, Haldwani has 20 years of history in quality education and is ranked among the top 10 private institutes of India. It was established in 1999 by the Modern Academy Society that runs this institute.
